Payroll Components

Salary Structure

Our competitive salary structure is designed to attract and retain top talent in the renewable energy sector.

  • Basic Salary
  • House Rent Allowance (HRA)
  • Special Allowance
  • Performance Linked Incentives
  • Retention Bonus

Benefits & Deductions

Comprehensive benefits package with transparent deductions for statutory compliance.

  • Provident Fund (PF) - 12%
  • Employee State Insurance (ESI)
  • Professional Tax
  • Income Tax (TDS)
  • Gratuity Contributions

Payroll Schedule

Structured payroll calendar ensuring timely processing and disbursement.

  • Salary Calculation: 25th - 28th
  • Approval Process: 29th - 30th
  • Salary Disbursement: 1st of Month
  • Payslip Distribution: 2nd of Month
  • Query Resolution: 3rd - 5th of Month

Payroll Process Timeline

4

Processing

Generating payroll registers and initiating bank transfers.

5

Distribution

Disbursing salaries and distributing payslips to employees.
